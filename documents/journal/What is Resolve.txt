🛡️ RESOLVE (RES) — SYSTEM DEFINITION
──────────────────────────────────────────────

**Stat Type:** Mental / Stability
**Soft Cap:** 10 (human), 20+ (augmented/post-human)
**Primary Role:** Governs an actor’s resistance to psychological disruption, emotional collapse, social coercion, or identity breakdown under pressure.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
🧩 What RES Is

RES models the *stability* and *durability* of an actor’s internal structure. It determines how well they maintain focus, control, and personal continuity when exposed to:

* Intimidation, interrogation, or manipulation
* Betrayal or social collapse
* Emotional shock, grief, fear, or public failure
* Demands to violate internal values or alignment

RES is **never an action**. It is a **passive buffer** — a foundation for withstanding effects that erode or disrupt the self.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
📐 System Area Influence

| System Area    | Role of RES                                        |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Control        | Maintain emotional regulation and physical poise   |
| Manipulation   | Resist being emotionally coerced or redirected     |
| Memory         | Resist trauma-induced memory degradation           |
| Interpretation | Maintain clarity under gaslighting, fear, or panic |

──────────────────────────────────────────────
⚙️ Runtime Effects

* Reduces **strain accumulation** under persona misalignment
* Buffers against forced leaks (emotional, informational, narrative)
* Mitigates collapse under high-pressure social techniques
* Raises resistance to:

  * Deceive, Interrogate, Dominate, Appeal
  * Social or alignment-based challenges
* May reduce **leakage frequency** (if supported by Mask)

──────────────────────────────────────────────
🔒 What RES Is Not

* Not a skill — RES is not trained or used directly
* Not a universal "willpower" — RES doesn’t make decisions
* Not a substitute for Mask — it works *with* Mask to buffer failure
* Not a health stat — physical damage has no RES mitigation

──────────────────────────────────────────────
🎯 Functional Summary

RES determines whether an actor *withstands pressure or fractures*.

It is the underlying mental chassis: the frame that carries persona, absorbs impact, and defines how long an identity can survive dissonance.

It does not make you right. It makes you *stay intact*.
